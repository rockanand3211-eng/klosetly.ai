Step 1: Joint & Landmark Detection (Pose Estimation)The Tech: Hum Google MediaPipe ya YOLOv8-Pose use karte hain.How it works: Jaise hi full-body image server par aati hai, AI human body par 33 key structural landmarks (jaise shoulders, hips, knees, ankles, aur elbows) ko trace karke ek skeletal map banata hai.The Purpose: Isse system ko body ka exact posture aur bone-structure alignment pata chal jata hai, chahe user thoda tedha hi kyun na khada ho.🔹 


Step 2: Background Stripping & Silhouette Masking (Semantic Segmentation)The Tech: Hum Meta ka SAM (Segment Anything Model) ya ek lightweight BiRefNet model use karte hain.How it works: AI human body ke exact edge pixels ko background (deewar, ghar, room) se bilkul alag (extract) kar deta hai.The Purpose: Background hatne ke baad jo bachti hai, wo hoti hai user ki pure solid black-and-white mask/silhouette binary image. Is shape ko dekh kar AI bata sakta hai ki user ka body type kya hai:Hourglass, Rectangle, Inverted Triangle, ya Pear shape.🔹 

Step 3: Proportional Measurement Mapping (Sizing & Fit Algorithm)The Logic: Ab bina kisi physical inch-tape ke exact size guess karne ke liye hum Relative Proportionality Math ka use karte hain.How it works:Skeletal map se hum shoulders aur hips ke beech ke pixel distance ka ratio nikalte hain.User onboarding ke time jo apni Height manually input karta hai, hum use use karte hain as a Reference Scaling Factor.Example Math: Agar height $175\text{ cm}$ hai aur image me pure head-to-toe pixel height $1000\text{ px}$ hai, toh iska matlab $1\text{ px} = 0.175\text{ cm}$. Is scaling factor se AI shoulder width, chest bounds, aur waist width ke pixels ko real centimeters/inches me mathematical precision ke sath convert kar leta hai.
