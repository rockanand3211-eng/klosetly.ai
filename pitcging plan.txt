🛝 Slide 2: The Core Problems (Dual-Threat Problem Statement)
Slide Title: The Modern Wardrobe Crisis

Bullet Points:

The Fit & Styling Dilemma: 80% of consumers struggle to find their exact style fit or match outfits efficiently, leading to high product returns and styling anxiety.

The Dead Wardrobe Burden: Millions of tons of high-quality clothes sit unworn in personal closets, with no seamless mechanism to list, declutter, or recycle them efficiently.

Key Stat Callout: "Average consumers wear only 20% of their wardrobe regularly, leaving 80% as dead capital."

🛝 Slide 3: The Solution (Introducing Closetly)
Slide Title: Enter Closetly: Your Virtual Ecosystem

Core Pillars:

Curated AI Stylist: A personalized computer vision module that takes user traits/photos to instantly design flawless outfit combinations from top brands.

The Circular Loop: An embedded, single-click checkout framework where retail items live side-by-side with individual peer-to-peer personal listings.

🛝 Slide 4: Unique Value Proposition (The Dynamic Grid Innovation)
Slide Title: Dual-Intent Interface Design

Layout Breakdown:

The 'Shop All' Grid: Seamless retail experience showing curated premium sets with an instant "Buy Now" checkout action.

The 'My Closet' Workspace: A digital twin of the user's real wardrobe. If an item is sitting idle, the interface displays an instant integrated "thriftIt" button to monetize old clothes instantly into the secondary marketplace ecosystem.

Data Points:

The Sustainability Pivot: Gen-Z and Millennial buyers are migrating rapidly toward circular fashion loops and conscious secondary marketplaces.

🛝 Slide 8: Business Model (How We Monetize)
Slide Title: The SaaS + Transaction Engine

Revenue Streams:

SaaS Tiers (Subscription): Basic wardrobe tracking is free. Unlimited virtual styling pipelines and premium '✨ AI Stylist' matching requires a monthly tier.

Marketplace Commission: Taking a small, fixed transaction commission on every user asset listed and sold via the "thriftIt" action network.

Brand Affiliate Partnerships: Revenue generated via contextual high-end fashion affiliate redirects.
